Sol Duc

Stay:

Sol Duc Campground is at the trailhead. There's 82 sites but will need to reserve a weekend way, way in advance. Will probably have better luck at Fairholme or Klahoya, which are both only 25-30 minutes away from the trailhead on US 101.

Do:

High divide loop is a steady climb up to Bogachiel Peak at 5478'. Little divide loop is climb to the top of the ridge 4150' high which you walk ontop for 3 miles.

Sunrise

Stay:

White River Campground is at the trailhead. There's XX sites but will need to reserve a weekend way, way in advance. Buck Creek Campground, Silver Springs Campground and The Dalles Campground are down on 410 and normally require reservations way in advance as well. There is plenty of free dispersed camping on Forest Road 73 as well as behind the airstrip on Forest Road 7160-210.

Do:

Hike the Burroughs Mountains for a loop of 10 miles, 3200'. . Little divide loop is climb to the top of the ridge 4150' high which you walk ontop for 3 miles.
