PNW Van Trips

Biking

Trips to all the best biking destinations in Washington. Single track and gravel options. Most gravel rides are created using Trailforks, Strava and RideWithMe heat maps but are primarily untested. They may be best ridden with suspension first before trying with gravel. Most rides start at the recommended campground which may add a little mileage.

Hiking

Trips to some of the best hikes in Washington. Tried to pick destinations with several hikes for multiple days. Ideally a hike will be longer, strenuous and a loop preferably. Some smaller hikes are routed as for a chiller, rest or recovery day.

Skiing

Planning a backcountry tour can be extrememly difficult and is heavily dependent on snow conditions. A major limiting factor depends on accessibility, this is why a lot of touring is done from ski resorts that are at the foor of high, aloine areas ideal for skiing. Washington ski resorts do allow van camping which is also ideal for an early start to a day of touring.
