Hurricane ridge

Stay:

Heart of the Hills Campground at the bottom Hurricane Ridge Road is open year around first come,firse serve with 105 campsites and a fee of $24. No electrical hook ups.

Do:

Hurricane Ridge is the primary location for skiing in the Olympics due to its accessibility. Park at the top of to start tours to Hurricane Ridge and Obstruction Point. The traverse to Hurricane Hill is 700 ft up, not much; add laps by skiing down and back up to the ridge where appealing. To the west, traverse along the closed obstruction point road for 7.5 miles to the foot of Obstruction Peak. Start a steeper climb to the top or traverse around to head towards Elk Mountain. Tour up to the East or West of Mount Angeles starting from pull out parking prior to the top of road.

Eat:

Bring own, no town nearby!
