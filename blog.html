Teanaway, May 2022

Stayed at Teanaway campground (no fee, no reservations). Would expect there to be almsot always room. First day, did a climb to the top of Main ridge and loped back down to the campsite on some beautiful single track. The gravel roads can be a bit loose making the climbs difficult. Most of the trails are exposed to the sun and can be quite hot in the summer, making the climbs gruleing. Reccommend sticking to the gravel for climbing vice single track. Incredible views of the Enchanmtents to the north once at the top of the ridge.

Little Naches, August 2022

Stayed at Little Naches Campground. Plenty of cmapsites and disperesed campging in the area, would expect there to be almsot always room. Rode up a very well kept forest road to the top of West Quartz. Singletrack downhill was flowy fun in sections, mostly rideable with a couple narrow, loose sections along the hill side ehich are best walked. Great views on the single track down.
