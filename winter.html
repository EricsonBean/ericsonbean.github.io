Hurricane Ridge provides easy access to touring into the Olympics. Mount Ellinor is perhaps the only other area for a day tour in the Olympics. I have not gotten the chance to do either, mostly because Snoqualmie has bit more to offer and is much less of a trek to get to. Both tours will have incredible views on a clear day. Conditions and snow can be more variable in the Olympics.
Ranier provides year round skiing at Paradise just 2 hours from seattle. Two years in a row I have done early season tours on Muir snowfields when I'm itching to get into ski season. Skiing on Ranier will normally be windy and icy, this is not a place to go from powdery turns. Just south of Ranier in the Tatoosh Range, great powder skiing can be found with less crowds than Snoqualmie. From the trailhead you can see Zipper and Fly Couloirs down the north face of Lane's Peak. To the east of Ranier is more to explore on Plummer and Pinnacle Peaks.
