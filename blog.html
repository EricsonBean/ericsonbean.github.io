Teanaway, May 2022

Stayed at Teanaway campground (no fee, no reservations). Would expect there to be almsot always room. First day, did a climb to the top of Main ridge and loped back down to the campsite on some beautiful single track. The gravel roads can be a bit loose making the climbs difficult. Most of the trails are exposed to the sun and can be quite hot in the summer, making the climbs gruleing. Reccommend sticking to the gravel for climbing vide single track. Incredible views of the Enchanmtents to the north once at the top of the ridge.
