Kendall Adventure Zone

Snoqualmie, WA

March 2023

We started our commonwelath approach to Kendall Adventure Zone at 730am. It took us 3 hrs to reach the entrance of the Twins Couloirs. There were a few peopel reaching KAZ the time we were but there were few tracks into the bowl. Jake took the skier's right couloir while I took the left. Conditions were incredible as we met up at th bottom of the Sisters to rip down the apron togehter. We skied to the skiers right to head towards the base of the most Eastern couloirs, Icy Gash and Silver Lining. We transitioned and skinned 2 hours to the top of Silver Lining. My friend skied Gash while I went back down Silver Lining. Once at the botttom, we headed back West to skin out of the "Not So Super Steep" Couloir. At this point we were both losing steam for our thrid climb but we had plnety of daylight left and perservered. We then has one last run through the popular Kendall Trees to get back to the car.
